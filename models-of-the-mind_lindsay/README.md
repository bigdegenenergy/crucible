# Models of the Mind: How Physics, Engineering and Mathematics Have Shaped Our Understanding of the Brain

**Author(s):** Grace Lindsay

**Category:** Computational Neuroscience

---

## Why This Book?

Traces how mathematical models have helped understand the brain - from neurons to memory, perception, movement, and decision-making. Covers information theory, network theory, Bayesian inference, and the origins of artificial neural networks.
