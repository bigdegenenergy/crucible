# Make It Stick: The Science of Successful Learning

**Author(s):** Peter C. Brown, Henry L. Roediger III, Mark A. McDaniel

**Category:** Learning Science

---

## Why This Book?

Synthesizes cognitive psychology research on effective learning. Covers self-testing, spaced repetition, interleaving, and embracing difficulty - evidence-based techniques for durable learning.
